Option DuckDuckGo :

Privacy First, Bult-in Tracker Scanner & Fire Button (deletes every Tab)
Option Cromite : 

My personal favorite. Built with Chromium, enhanced Privacy settings with integrated AdBlock. Replaces the "Bromite" project.
